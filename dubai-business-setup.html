Dubai Business Setup Process

Follow our proven 7-step process to establish your Dubai business efficiently and compliantly.

1

Choose Business Type

Select between mainland, free zone, or offshore based on your business needs and activities.

2

Select Activities

Define your business activities and ensure they align with your chosen jurisdiction's regulations.

3

Reserve Trade Name

Submit your preferred company name for approval and reservation with relevant authorities.

4

Prepare Documents

Compile and notarize all required documents including passports, photos, and business plans.

5

Submit Application

File your license application with the relevant authority and pay required government fees.

6

Secure Office Space

Arrange suitable office space - physical office, flexi-desk, or virtual office as required.

7

Receive License

Collect your trade license and begin business operations. Apply for visas and bank accounts.
