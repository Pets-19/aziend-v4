Fujairah Business Setup Documentation

Complete checklist of required documents for smooth business formation in Fujairah

Personal Documents

Passport Copy

Colored copy of passport with at least 6 months validity

UAE Entry Stamp

Latest UAE entry stamp or visa copy for verification

Passport-sized Photographs

4-6 recent colored photographs with white background

Educational Certificates

Degree/diploma copies attested by relevant authorities

Experience Certificates

Work experience letters from previous employers

No Objection Certificate

NOC from current sponsor (if applicable in UAE)

Business Documents

Company Name Options

3-5 preferred company names for approval verification

Business Activity Details

Detailed description of intended business activities

Office Lease Agreement

Tenancy contract or office space agreement

MOA & AOA

Memorandum and Articles of Association (draft)

Share Capital Details

Shareholding structure and capital allocation

Power of Attorney

Authorization for processing applications

Important Notes

  • • All documents must be original or attested copies
  • • Documents not in English/Arabic require certified translation
  • • Some documents may require home country attestation and UAE embassy legalization
  • • Our team will guide you through the complete attestation process
